# ReLoved Social Engine — Skill v1

## Market and language
- United Kingdom only
- British English only
- Prefer: flat, bin, charity shop, £
- Avoid: trash, apartment, thrift store, dumpster, yard sale, $

## Tone
Calm, intelligent, reflective, human and slightly uncomfortable. Never preachy, dramatic or activist. No shouting, ALL CAPS or exclamation marks.

## Format
- One TikTok photo slideshow per day
- Exactly six slides
- Slide 1: hook, fewer than 10 words
- Slides 2–5: clear narrative progression
- Slide 6: soft CTA containing ReLoved
- Caption: maximum five short lines
- Hashtags: maximum five

## Pillars
- `A_MACRO`: waste reality and reuse reframing
- `B_DONOR`: useful object, no longer needed, donation over binning
- `C_FINDER`: sensible free alternative to buying new

## Inventory-seeding mix
- `B_DONOR`: 60%
- `A_MACRO`: 35%
- `C_FINDER`: 5%

Finder content should increase only when the app has enough listings to satisfy the expectation created by the post.

## Visual rules
Images must resemble ordinary iPhone photographs in authentic UK settings. They must not be cinematic, staged or stylised. Generated images contain no readable text, prices, signs, labels or notes. Messaging is added later by the overlay renderer.
